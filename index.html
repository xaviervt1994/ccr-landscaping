Frequently Asked Questions

How much does lawn mowing cost?

Our lawn mowing starts at $55/visit for weekly service or $65/visit for biweekly service (lots ‚â§5,000 sq ft). Larger properties are priced based on size and complexity. We provide transparent, upfront pricing with no hidden fees.

Do you offer same-day service?

Yes! We reserve 2 priority slots daily for urgent needs. Same-day service is available at a 50-100% premium. If our priority slots are full, we can schedule you for the next available standard service slot.

What's included in basic lawn service?

All our maintenance plans include professional mowing, precision edging along walkways and driveways, and complete blow-off of hardscapes. Additional services like shrub trimming, pet waste removal, and gutter cleaning are available as add-ons.

What areas do you serve?

We proudly serve Austin, Round Rock, Pflugerville, Cedar Park, Lakeway, Bee Cave, Leander, and Georgetown. Don't see your area? Give us a call‚Äîwe may still be able to help or add your neighborhood to our route!

What happens if it rains?

Safety first! We'll reschedule your service within 48 hours of the rain delay. We'll notify you of any weather-related changes and work with you to find the best makeup time.

Are you licensed and insured?

Absolutely! CCR Landscaping is fully licensed and insured for your protection and peace of mind. We're happy to provide proof of insurance upon request.
